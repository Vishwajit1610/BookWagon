Deep Work

Author: Cal Newport

Published: 2016

Category: Productivity / Self-help

Deep Work is a guide to focused success in a distracted world. Cal Newport explains why cultivating the ability to concentrate without distraction is becoming increasingly rare, yet incredibly valuable in our economy. Through practical advice and examples, the book shows how to master deep work to achieve more meaningful results.
